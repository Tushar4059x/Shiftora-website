## HR Automation: Streamline Hiring, Onboarding, and Beyond

HR professionals spend 73% of their time on administrative tasks. Paperwork, scheduling, data entry—none of it requires human judgment.

Let's automate it.

### HR Processes to Automate

**Recruiting:**
- Job posting distribution
- Resume screening
- Interview scheduling
- Candidate communication
- Reference checks

**Onboarding:**
- Offer letter generation
- Document collection
- System provisioning
- Training assignment
- First-day scheduling

**Employee Management:**
- Time-off requests
- Performance reviews
- Benefits enrollment
- Policy acknowledgments
- Training tracking

**Offboarding:**
- Exit interview scheduling
- Asset collection tracking
- System deprovisioning
- Final paperwork
- Knowledge transfer

### Recruitment Automation

**Job Posting Automation:**
- Post once → Distribute everywhere
- Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, etc.
- Consistent branding
- Automatic refresh

**Resume Screening:**
- AI parses resumes
- Scores against job requirements
- Ranks candidates automatically
- Surfaces best matches

**Interview Scheduling:**
- Candidate self-schedules
- Calendar availability checked
- Confirmation and reminders sent
- Panel coordination automated

**Candidate Communication:**
- Application received → Thank you
- Moving forward → Next steps
- Not selected → Respectful rejection
- All templates personalized

**Reference Checks:**
- Automated email requests
- Standard question forms
- Reminders for non-response
- Results compiled automatically

### Onboarding Automation

**Day -7 (Before Start):**
- Offer letter generated and sent
- Benefits info and enrollment
- Tax forms (W-4, I-9 prep)
- Equipment request triggered
- Building access requested

**Day -1:**
- Welcome email with Day 1 info
- Team notification
- Desk/workspace confirmed
- Manager prep reminder

**Day 1:**
- System accounts created
- Training schedule sent
- First-day checklist
- Introduction emails

**Week 1:**
- Daily check-in surveys
- Training progress tracked
- Mentor assignments
- Goals and expectations

**Day 30/60/90:**
- Automated check-in surveys
- Manager review reminders
- Performance conversation prompts
- Probation tracking

### Time-Off Automation

**Request Flow:**
- Employee submits via system
- Manager notified for approval
- Auto-approve within policy
- Calendar updated
- Team notified
- Payroll adjusted

**Tracking:**
- Balances calculated automatically
- Accruals processed
- Carryover handled
- Reports generated

### Performance Review Automation

**Annual/Quarterly Reviews:**
- Reminders sent at cycle start
- Self-assessment distributed
- Manager forms triggered
- 360 feedback collected
- Review meetings scheduled
- Documents compiled

**Continuous Feedback:**
- Monthly check-in prompts
- Goal progress tracking
- Recognition automation
- Issue escalation

## Accounting Automation: Bookkeeping on Autopilot

Every hour spent on manual bookkeeping is an hour stolen from growing your business.

Modern accounting automation handles the tedious work so you don't have to.

### What Can Be Automated?

**Data Entry:**
- Bank transactions import
- Invoice data extraction
- Receipt capture
- Expense logging

**Categorization:**
- Transaction classification
- Expense coding
- Account mapping
- Department assignment

**Reconciliation:**
- Bank matching
- Invoice payment matching
- Statement reconciliation
- Discrepancy flagging

**Reporting:**
- Financial statements
- Cash flow reports
- Budget vs. actual
- Tax preparation

### Core Accounting Automations

**1. Bank Feed Automation**
- Transactions sync automatically
- Rules categorize recurring items
- AI learns your patterns
- Exceptions flagged for review

**2. Invoice Processing**
- Invoices emailed to dedicated address
- AI extracts all data
- Entry created automatically
- Approval workflow triggered

**3. Expense Management**
- Mobile receipt capture
- OCR extraction
- Auto-categorization
- Policy compliance check

**4. Bill Payment**
- Approval workflows
- Scheduled payments
- Payment method optimization
- Confirmation tracking

**5. Reporting**
- Scheduled financial reports
- Real-time dashboards
- Variance alerts
- Tax-ready exports

## Reporting Automation: Never Manually Build a Report Again

Every week, someone on your team spends hours pulling data, making spreadsheets, and creating reports.

Then next week, they do it all again.

This is madness. Let's fix it.

### The Manual Reporting Problem

**Time wasted:**
- Logging into systems: 15 min
- Exporting data: 20 min
- Cleaning and organizing: 30 min
- Building visualizations: 45 min
- Writing insights: 30 min
- Formatting and sending: 20 min
- **Total: 2-3 hours per report**

**And then:**
- Questions about data require re-pulling
- Numbers don't match between reports
- Historical comparisons are painful
- Nobody actually reads the whole thing

### Types of Reports to Automate

**Executive Dashboards:**
- Real-time KPIs
- Key metrics at a glance
- Trend visualization
- Drill-down capability

**Sales Reports:**
- Pipeline status
- Rep performance
- Deal velocity
- Win/loss analysis

**Marketing Reports:**
- Campaign performance
- Channel attribution
- Lead generation
- Content engagement

**Financial Reports:**
- Revenue tracking
- Expense analysis
- Cash flow
- Budget vs. actual

**Operational Reports:**
- Process metrics
- Efficiency tracking
- SLA compliance
- Resource utilization

### Building Automated Dashboards

**Step 1: Define What Matters**
- Identify 5-10 key metrics
- Determine update frequency
- Define audience needs
- Set target values

**Step 2: Connect Data Sources**
Common sources:
- CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- Accounting (QuickBooks, Xero)
- Marketing (Google Analytics, Facebook)
- Custom databases

**Step 3: Design Dashboards**
Best practices:
- Most important metrics at top
- Comparison to targets/previous
- Clear visualizations
- Minimal clutter

**Step 4: Set Up Delivery**
Options:
- Email scheduled reports
- Slack notifications
- SMS for critical alerts
- Mobile app access

### Automated Alerts

**Don't wait for reports—get alerted:**

**Revenue alerts:**
- Daily sales below X
- Unusual drop in conversions
- Big deal closed

**Operations alerts:**
- Process failure
- SLA breached
- Inventory critical

**Marketing alerts:**
- Campaign underperforming
- Traffic spike
- Negative mentions

## How Much Does Business Automation Cost? Complete Pricing Guide

"How much will automation cost?" is the first question everyone asks.

Here's a transparent breakdown—no hidden surprises.

### Cost Components

**1. Software/Platform Costs**
Monthly or annual subscriptions for automation tools.

**2. Implementation Costs**
One-time setup, configuration, and customization.

**3. Integration Costs**
Connecting systems together.

**4. Maintenance Costs**
Ongoing updates, fixes, and optimization.

**5. Training Costs**
Getting your team up to speed.

### DIY Automation Costs

**Using Platform Native Tools:**
| Platform | Automation Features | Monthly Cost |
|----------|---------------------|--------------|
| HubSpot | Marketing + Sales | Free-$50/mo |
| Shopify | E-commerce | $29-299/mo |
| QuickBooks | Accounting | $30/mo |
| Mailchimp | Email | Free-$20/mo |

**Using Integration Platforms:**
| Tool | Tasks/Month | Monthly Cost |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| Zapier | 750 | $20 |
| Make | 10,000 ops | $9 |
| n8n Cloud | 2,500 exec | $20 |

**DIY Total:** $50-500/month + your time (10-40 hrs setup)

### Hiring an Automation Agency

**Typical Project Costs:**

| Project Type | Cost Range | Timeframe |
|--------------|------------|-----------|
| Simple workflow (5-10 steps) | $500-2,000 | 1 week |
| Complex workflow | $2,000-10,000 | 2-4 weeks |
| Multi-system integration | $5,000-25,000 | 4-8 weeks |
| Custom AI solution | $10,000-50,000+ | 8-16 weeks |
| Enterprise transformation | $50,000-200,000+ | 3-12 months |

**Ongoing Support:**
| Level | Includes | Monthly Cost |
|-------|----------|--------------|
| Basic | Issue fixes, updates | $200-500 |
| Standard | + Optimization, support | $500-2,000 |
| Premium | + Strategy, development | $2,000-5,000 |

### Cost by Use Case

**Email Automation:**
- DIY: $0-50/mo + 5 hrs setup
- Agency: $500-2,000 + $0-100/mo

**Lead Capture + CRM:**
- DIY: $20-100/mo + 10 hrs setup
- Agency: $1,000-5,000 + $50-200/mo

**E-commerce Automation:**
- DIY: $100-500/mo + 40+ hrs setup
- Agency: $5,000-20,000 + $200-500/mo

**Full Business Automation:**
- DIY: $300-1,000/mo + hundreds of hours
- Agency: $15,000-100,000 + $500-2,000/mo

### ROI Perspective

**The Real Question:** What does it cost NOT to automate?

**Example: Invoice Processing**
- Manual: 2 hrs/week × $30/hr = $3,120/year
- Automated: $1,500 setup + $20/mo = $1,740/year 1, $240/year 2+
- **ROI: 81% Year 1, 92% Year 2+**

### Getting Value at Every Budget

**Budget: $0-100/mo (DIY)**

- Learn no-code platforms
- Focus on high-impact, simple automations
- Accept longer implementation time

## Sales Automation: Close More Deals with Less Effort

Sales reps spend only 36% of their time actually selling. The rest is admin, data entry, and manual tasks.

What if you could flip that? Here's how sales automation makes it happen.

### What is Sales Automation?

Sales automation uses technology to handle repetitive sales tasks:
- Lead data entry
- Follow-up sequences
- Meeting scheduling
- Pipeline updates
- Proposal generation
- Activity logging

This frees reps to do what they do best: sell.

### Key Sales Automations

**Lead Capture:**
- Web forms → CRM instantly
- Email parser → Extract lead details
- LinkedIn → Sync connections
- Enrichment → Add company data automatically

**Outreach:**
- Personalized email sequences
- Multi-channel cadences
- Automatic follow-ups
- Response detection

**Meeting Scheduling:**
- Self-book calendar links
- Automatic reminders
- Conference call setup
- Prep materials sent

**Pipeline Management:**
- Auto-stage updates
- Task creation
- Stale deal alerts
- Forecast calculation

**Post-Sale:**
- Handoff to success team
- Onboarding triggers
- Upsell sequences
- Renewal reminders

**Week 1: Lead Management**

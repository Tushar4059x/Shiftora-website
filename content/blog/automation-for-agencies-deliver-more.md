## Automation for Agencies: Deliver More Without More Staff

Agency math is brutal: revenue scales with headcount, but margins stay flat.

Automation changes the equation. Deliver more without proportionally more staff.

### Agency Processes to Automate

**Client Operations:**
- Onboarding sequences
- Reporting automation
- Status update delivery
- Feedback collection

**Project Management:**
- Template project creation
- Task assignment
- Deadline tracking
- Resource allocation

**Sales & Marketing:**
- Lead qualification
- Proposal generation
- Contract management
- Invoice automation

**Internal:**
- Time tracking
- Capacity planning
- Knowledge management
- Team communication

### Key Agency Automations

**1. Client Onboarding Automation**
- Contract signed → Trigger onboarding
- Questionnaire sent automatically
- Tasks created from template
- Team notified with context
- Welcome sequence delivered

**2. Reporting Automation**
- Data pulls automatically
- Reports generated
- Scheduled delivery
- Client portal updated

**3. Sales Pipeline**
- Lead → Qualify → Propose → Close
- Proposals generated from templates
- Contracts sent for signature
- Won → Onboarding triggered

**4. Project Templating**
- New project → Apply template
- All tasks pre-built
- Assignments based on role
- Milestones automated

## How to Choose an AI Automation Agency: The Complete Checklist

Hiring the wrong automation partner can cost you months and tens of thousands of dollars. The right partner can transform your business.

This guide helps you tell the difference.

### Why Work with an Agency vs. DIY?

**When to DIY:**
- Simple automations (Zapier, Make)
- Technical team in-house
- Plenty of time to learn
- Small budget

**When to hire an agency:**
- Complex, multi-system workflows
- Custom AI/ML requirements
- Time is more valuable than money
- Need reliability and support
- Strategic guidance needed

### Questions to Ask Every Agency

**About Their Experience:**

1. "How many automation projects have you completed?"
2. "What's your experience with [specific tool/platform]?"
3. "Can you share case studies from similar businesses?"
4. "What was your most complex implementation?"
5. "Who will actually be working on my project?"

**About Their Process:**

6. "How do you approach new projects?"
7. "What information do you need from us?"
8. "How long does a typical project take?"
9. "How do you handle scope changes?"
10. "What does testing and QA look like?"

**About Business Impact:**

11. "How do you measure success?"
12. "What ROI have similar clients seen?"
13. "How quickly can we expect to see results?"
14. "What risks should we be aware of?"

**About After Launch:**

15. "What happens after the project is complete?"
16. "How is ongoing maintenance handled?"
17. "What if something breaks?"
18. "Can we make changes ourselves?"
19. "What documentation will we receive?"

**About Pricing:**

20. "How is pricing structured?"
21. "Are there ongoing costs?"
22. "What's included vs. extra?"
23. "How do you handle overruns?"

### Pricing Models Explained

**Fixed Price:**
- Set price for defined scope
- Best for: Clear, well-defined projects
- Risk: Scope creep battles

**Hourly/Time & Materials:**
- Pay for time spent
- Best for: Exploratory or evolving projects
- Risk: Budget uncertainty

**Monthly Retainer:**
- Ongoing relationship, set monthly fee
- Best for: Continuous optimization
- Risk: Paying when little work needed

**Value-Based:**
- Price tied to results delivered
- Best for: High-confidence, measurable outcomes
- Risk: Misaligned incentives

**Hybrid:**
- Fixed price for build, retainer for support
- Often the best of both worlds

### The Selection Process

**Step 1: Create a Shortlist (Week 1)**
- Research 5-10 potential agencies
- Review portfolios and case studies
- Check reviews and testimonials

**Step 2: Initial Conversations (Week 2)**
- Schedule discovery calls
- Use the questions above
- Assess communication quality

**Step 3: Proposal Review (Week 3)**
- Request detailed proposals
- Compare approaches, not just prices
- Check for completeness

**Step 4: Reference Checks (Week 3-4)**
- Talk to past clients
- Ask about challenges, not just successes
- Verify claimed results

**Step 5: Final Decision (Week 4)**
- Trust your gut on cultural fit
- Consider long-term partnership potential
- Start with a smaller project if possible
